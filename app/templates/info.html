ABOUT

What is Notella?

Notella is a student-focused study platform, combining note-taking with markdown toolbar, flashcard decks, and AI generated quizzes into a single workspace. Built to help students study easier.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Is my data private?

Yes, your notes and flashcard decks are private by default. You can choose what to make public.

Can I copy someone else's notes?

Yes, you can copy and personalise any public notes and flashcard decks.

How does the AI quiz work?

The AI reads your note and automatically generates multiple choice questions. Results are not 100% accurate.

Are my quiz results saved?

Yes, AI quiz results can be saved or discarded. However, the flashcard session results are not saved. They are just for self checking.
